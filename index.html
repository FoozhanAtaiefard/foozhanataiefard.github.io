Foozhan Ataiefard

I'm a senior ML researcher focused on deep learning systems and applied research across vision-language models, computer vision, graphics, large language models, CUDA optimization, and reinforcement learning. I currently work at NVIDIA as a Senior DevTech Engineer / Deep Learning Researcher, where I work on generative models, diffusion-based rendering, model optimization, and AI for games.

Previously, I was a Senior AI Researcher at Ascend Research, where I accelerated large-model training on GPU and NPU clusters using quantization, LoRA, parallelism, and communication compression, and optimized CUDA kernels for custom PyTorch operators. Before that, I worked with Oracle Labs on RLHF for SQL generation, with Intelius AI and the University of Calgary on robust deep reinforcement learning, and with Itoll on production computer vision and OCR systems.
