Foozhan Ataiefard

I am a senior research engineer working on VLM and LLM efficiency, diffusion-based rendering, and world models. I currently work at NVIDIA as a DevTech Engineer.

Prior to joining NVIDIA, I was a Senior AI Researcher at Ascend AI Research, where I worked on LLM and CV models training and inference efficiency across GPU and NPU clusters. Before that, I was a student researcher at Oracle Labs working on Reinforcement Learning for code generation, and RL-based trading agents at Intelius AI. I've had the privilege of working with Prof. Hadi Hemmati, Niel Walkinshaw during my time at University of Calgary.

I am also interested in any form of research collaboration.
